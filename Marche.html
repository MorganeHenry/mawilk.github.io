Market days in and around Carnac

One of the largest markets in the region is Carnac!

  • Monday: Auray
  • Tuesday: La Trinité sur Mer, Locmariaquer, Etel
  • Wednesday: Vannes, Lorient
  • Thursday: Saint-Pierre Quiberon, Erdeven, Hennebont,(the Carnac night market)
  • Friday: La Trinité sur Mer, Plouharnel, Auray
  • Saturday: Quiberon, Vannes, Lorient, Locmariaquer
  • Sunday: Carnac
